    Let's Start!

    Combine brown sugar and water in small saucepan.

    Bring to gentle boil and cook, stirring often, until it reaches 240 degrees.

    Mix Ingredients!

    Stir in butter, salt, vinegar and 1/4 cup of heavy cream.

    Let cool to room temperature and stir in rum extract.

    Keep Mixing and Stirring!

    Combine 2 tbsp of brown sugar mixture with 1/2 cup of heavy cream.

    Beat with electric mixer for 2 or 3 minutes, until thickened but not completely whipped.

    Divide into Cups!

    To serve, divide remaining brown sugar mixture between 4 tall glasses.

    Cream Soda makes a Dazzling Entrance!

    Add 1/4 cup cream soda to each glass, stirring to combine.

    Top Up and Enjoy!

    Fill each glass nearly to the top with the additional soda.

    Spoon whipped topping over each.
